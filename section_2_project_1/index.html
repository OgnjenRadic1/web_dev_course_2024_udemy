The Best Movies

My Top 3 movies of all-time.

The Jesus Film (1979)

A landmark film depicting the life of Jesus Christ based on the Gospel of Luke,
renowned for its faithful adaptation and worldwide distribution.

Ben Hur (1959)

An epic historical drama that follows the journey of Judah Ben-Hur,
a Jewish prince who is betrayed and seeks redemption amidst the
backdrop of Roman-occupied Jerusalem.

Kingdom of Heaven (2005)

A historical epic set during the Crusades,
focusing on a blacksmith named Balian who finds himself
embroiled in political intrigue and warfare in the Holy Land,
while grappling with questions of faith and honor.
